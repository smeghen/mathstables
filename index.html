Maths Tables

Games to help you learn the 1 to 12 tables for addition, subtraction, multiplication and division.

Info

Maths Tables is a game for learning and practicing the Addition, Subtraction, Multiplication and Division tables, that are taught as the foundation of mathematics in schools.

The game works by first selecting the equation type: Addition, Subtraction, Multiplication or Division.

Next select the table number you want to use: 1 to 12.

The game will then select random questions based on your selection and test to see how well you know your tables.

Best of luck and enjoy.
